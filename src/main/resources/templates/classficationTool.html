Rubbish Classfication Tool

This is a tool helps you figure out which bin should the rubbish goes.

The Green bin also called FOGO(food organics, garden organics) bin, accept all organic rubbish including food and plants. Please put your food rubbish here if you got a green bin.

The Red bin accepts most things not included in the other bins, which include Nappies, Plastic straws, Toothpaste tubes & brushes and Food wrappers.

The yellow-lidded bins accept recyclable materials including paper and cardboard, metal and aluminium. cans and plastic containers which contain recycling codes.

*Notice that electronic rubbish should not be put in your rubbish or recycling bin. Take them to a drop-off point please. To find your closest e-waste drop-off point, use the Recycling Near You directory
